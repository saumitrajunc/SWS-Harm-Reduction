Policy Updates

Important changes and updates to campus policies and procedures.

Recent Policy Changes

  • Good Samaritan Policy: Updated to provide clearer protections for students seeking help
  • Narcan Access Policy: Expanded to include all campus buildings and facilities
  • Training Requirements: New requirements for residence hall staff
  • Confidentiality Protections: Enhanced privacy protections for students
  • Emergency Response: Updated protocols for overdose situations
  • Volunteer Policies: New guidelines for peer educators and volunteers
