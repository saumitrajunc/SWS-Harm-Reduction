Saving Lives Through Harm Reduction

The UNC Chapel Hill Harm Reduction Initiative is dedicated to preventing opioid-related deaths by making Narcan (naloxone) accessible across our campus community. We believe that every life matters and that harm reduction saves lives.

Get Narcan Now Learn More

Our Mission

As part of the Student Wellness and Safety Department of Student Government, we are committed to creating a safer campus environment by providing education, resources, and life-saving Narcan to prevent opioid overdose deaths in our community.

Narcan Distribution

Free Narcan kits available at multiple locations across campus, including residence halls, student centers, and health services.

Education & Training

Comprehensive training sessions on overdose recognition, Narcan administration, and harm reduction principles for students and staff.

Community Support

Building a supportive community that reduces stigma around substance use and promotes evidence-based harm reduction strategies.

Where to Find Narcan

  • Student Health Services (Main Campus)
  • Student Union Information Desk
  • Davis Library (24/7 Access)
  • Undergraduate Library
  • Wilson Library
  • Campus Recreation Centers
  • Emergency Blue Light Stations

Narcan is FREE and available to all students, faculty, and staff. No questions asked.
