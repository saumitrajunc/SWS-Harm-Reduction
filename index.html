Saving Lives Through Harm Reduction

The UNC Chapel Hill Harm Reduction Initiative is dedicated to preventing opioid-related deaths by making Narcan (naloxone) accessible across our campus community. We believe that every life matters and that harm reduction saves lives.

Get Narcan Now

Our Mission

As part of the Student Wellness and Safety Department of Student Government, we are committed to creating a safer campus environment by providing education, resources, and life-saving Narcan to prevent opioid overdose deaths in our community.
