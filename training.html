What You'll Learn

Our training covers essential harm reduction knowledge and practical skills.

Training Topics

  • Overdose Recognition: Identifying signs and symptoms
  • Narcan Administration: Intranasal and intramuscular methods
  • Emergency Response: Calling 911 and staying with the person
  • Harm Reduction Principles: Evidence-based practices
  • Stigma Reduction: Creating supportive environments
  • Safe Use Practices: Risk reduction strategies
  • Peer Support: Supporting others in recovery
  • Legal Information: Good Samaritan laws and rights

Certification & Recognition

Complete our training programs and receive official certification.

Certification Levels

  • Basic Certification: Complete basic Narcan training (1 hour)
  • Peer Educator: Complete intensive training program (6 hours)
  • Trainer Certification: Learn to train others (8 hours)
  • Advanced Training: Specialized topics and scenarios (4 hours)

Benefits of Certification

  • Official certificate of completion
  • Priority access to advanced training
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Continuing education credits
