Our Mission & Vision

The UNC Harm Reduction Initiative operates under the Student Wellness & Safety Department at UNC Chapel Hill. Our mission is to reduce harm associated with opioid use, educate campus community members, and save lives by ensuring free and confidential access to naloxone (Narcan), overdose prevention training, and support resources.
