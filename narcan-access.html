How to Get a Narcan Kit

Getting a Narcan kit is simple and confidential. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Find a Location

Visit any of our campus access points listed above. All locations are clearly marked and staff are trained to help.

Step 2: Ask for a Kit

Simply ask for a Narcan kit. No questions will be asked about why you need it. The process takes less than 2 minutes.

Step 3: Receive Training

Staff will provide brief instructions on how to use Narcan safely. You can also request additional training materials.

Step 4: Take Action

You're now equipped to save a life. Remember: if you use Narcan, always call 911 immediately.

How to Use Narcan

Narcan (naloxone) is easy to use and can save a life. Here's what you need to know:

Signs of Overdose

  • Unresponsive or unconscious
  • Slow, shallow, or no breathing
  • Blue or gray lips/fingernails
  • Pinpoint pupils
  • Cold, clammy skin
  • Snoring or gurgling sounds

Steps to Administer Narcan

  • Call 911 immediately
  • Check for breathing and pulse
  • Administer Narcan (intranasal or intramuscular)
  • Perform rescue breathing if needed
  • Stay with the person until help arrives
  • Administer second dose if no response in 3-5 minutes

Legal Information & Policies

Understanding your rights and protections when using Narcan.

Good Samaritan Laws

North Carolina's Good Samaritan law protects individuals who seek medical help for drug overdoses, including those who administer Narcan.

Confidentiality

All interactions with our program are confidential. We do not share information with law enforcement or other parties.

Campus Policy

UNC supports harm reduction efforts and encourages students to seek help during medical emergencies without fear of disciplinary action.

Emergency Protocol

Always call 911 first. Narcan is not a substitute for professional medical care, but it can buy time until help arrives.
